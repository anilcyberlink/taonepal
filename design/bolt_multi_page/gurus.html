Our Gurus & Masters

Guiding lights on the path of Tao

Master Surya Bahadur Thapa

Founder & Chief Spiritual Guide

Master Surya has dedicated over 40 years to the study and practice of Taoist philosophy. Trained at Wudang Mountains in China, he brings authentic teachings to Nepal...

Master Surya Bahadur Thapa dedicated his life to the study and practice of Taoist philosophy after a transformative journey to China in 1985. Trained under renowned Taoist masters at Wudang Mountains, he returned to Nepal with a vision to share these ancient teachings with his homeland.

With over 40 years of practice and 30 years of teaching experience, Master Thapa has guided thousands of students on their spiritual journey. His teachings emphasize the practical application of Taoist principles in modern life.

"The Tao is not a destination but a way of walking. Every step taken with awareness is a step towards harmony with the universe."

Key Teachings:

  • Wu Wei (effortless action) in daily life
  • Qi Gong for health and longevity
  • Meditation for inner peace
  • Harmony with nature's rhythms

Master Chen Wei

Senior Teacher & Philosopher

Master Chen specializes in Taoist philosophy and classical texts. Born in Beijing, he brings deep knowledge of the Tao Te Ching and I Ching...

Master Chen Wei specializes in Taoist philosophy and classical texts. Born in Beijing, he brings deep knowledge of the Tao Te Ching, I Ching, and Zhuangzi to our community. His scholarly approach makes ancient wisdom accessible to modern practitioners.

After completing his PhD in Chinese Philosophy, Master Chen spent 15 years in monasteries across China before joining our association. He teaches that philosophy is not meant to be abstract but lived every day.

"True understanding comes not from reading about the Tao, but from experiencing it in each breath, each step, each moment."

Specializations:

  • Classical Taoist texts interpretation
  • Philosophy discussion circles
  • Calligraphy as meditation
  • I Ching divination practices

Master Liu Xiang

Qi Gong & Healing Master

Master Liu is renowned for his expertise in Qi Gong and Taoist healing arts. With 35 years of practice, he has helped thousands restore health and vitality...

Master Liu Xiang is renowned for his expertise in Qi Gong and Taoist healing arts. With 35 years of practice, he has helped thousands restore health and vitality through energy cultivation techniques. His gentle yet powerful approach makes healing accessible to all.

Trained in traditional Chinese medicine and Taoist internal alchemy, Master Liu combines ancient wisdom with modern understanding of health and wellness. His sessions integrate movement, breathwork, and meditation for holistic healing.

"Qi flows where attention goes. By cultivating awareness of our life energy, we unlock the body's natural capacity for healing."

Areas of Expertise:

  • Medical Qi Gong therapy
  • Internal alchemy practices
  • Five Element healing
  • Taoist longevity exercises

Master Karma Dorje

Meditation & Mindfulness Teacher

Master Karma bridges Taoist and Buddhist meditation practices. Nepali-born, he studied in Tibet and China, bringing unique perspectives to our community...

Master Karma Dorje bridges Taoist and Buddhist meditation practices. Nepali-born, he studied in Tibet and China, bringing unique perspectives to our community. His teaching style is warm, accessible, and deeply grounded in personal experience.

Having practiced for 30 years, Master Karma emphasizes that meditation is not separate from life but woven into every moment. His retreats are known for creating deep transformation while remaining joyful and light.

"Meditation is simply returning home to yourself, again and again, with kindness and without judgment."

Teaching Focus:

  • Sitting and walking meditation
  • Mindfulness in daily activities
  • Retreat facilitation
  • Integration of practice into modern life
