Temple Construction Projects

Building sacred spaces for future generations

Our temple construction projects create dedicated spaces for meditation, study, and community gathering. Each temple is designed with traditional Taoist architectural principles, harmonizing with the natural environment.

Flagship Project

Kathmandu Main Temple

Our flagship temple in Sitapaila, Kathmandu, is designed to accommodate 500+ practitioners. The three-story structure will feature a grand meditation hall, library, classroom spaces, and residential quarters for visiting masters.
